About Yuvaan

The youth who were inspired by Swami Vivekananda, Indiaâ€™s ex-President Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam and guided by Gandhian Dr. S. N. Subbarao have come together to form the voluntary youth organization, Yuvaan on November 1, 2013.

Yuvaan provides underprivileged including orphan, nomadic and tribal students educational fees, material, career guidance, vocational skills training, counselling and lodging and meals as the need be free of cost.

Yuvaan also focuses its activities to inculcate social sensitivity, brotherhood, love for the country and a humane perspective without any discrimination.

Our mission is to provide educational support, career & employment guidance, vocational skills training, lodging & meals free of cost to socially and economically underprivileged & orphaned students.

Our focus of activities is to inculcate self-respect, confidence, social sensitivity, brotherhood & love for the country. Every youth should have an equal opportunity for higher education.

Our goal is to build up the nation by strengthening character & nurture humane perspective without any discrimination. Every youth should have employment regardless of their social-economic status.

Explore all our programs

Youth Empowerment Centre

According to government rules, orphans have to leave the orphanages after they are 18 years old.

Their dreams remain unfulfilled as they step out into the world without any hope of support.

They are forced by their circumstances to take up any kind of job they get in order to earn their living even though they would like to take up higher education.

Many socially and economically underprivileged students are also deprived of equal opportunities of education and employment.

The Dr. S.N. Subbarao Youth Empowerment Centre provides full residential support to such young students.

Skill and Career Development Center

Centre for Skill Career Development is providing unemployed youth training & career guidance for employment in Government and Private sectors.

The Centre have well equipped Library & Study room facility.

The Library has 1500+ latest & excellent books on all types of Competitive exams including UPSC, MPSC, Police, Railway, Bank etc with several magazines & newspapers also.

The centre organizes inspiring lectures of UPSC, MPSC toppers and senior Government officials under PRERANA initiative.

There are thousands of students benefitted by the Prerana programs and given their life a better direction.

Yuvaan Sevavrati - Volunteering

Yuvaan is building up the youth and inculcating in them social sensitivity, love for the country and a humane perspective without any kind of discrimination.

The Yuvaan Sevavrati (Volunteers) program is aimed at bringing about positive change by channelizing the energy of youths.

Through this programs the youth are provided with opportunities to to contribute in various social activities as Yuvaan Volunteers.

Yuvaan Sevavrati enriches and deepens the awareness of social responsibilities among the youth and inspires them to lead a life of hope.
