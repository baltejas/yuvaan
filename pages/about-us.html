ABOUT US

The youth who were inspired by Swami Vivekananda, India’s ex-President Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam and guided by Gandhian Dr. S. N. Subbarao have come together to form the voluntary youth organization, Yuvaan on November 1, 2013. Yuvaan provides socially, economically underprivileged and orphaned students educational material, career guidance, vocational skills training and lodging and meals as the need be free of cost. Yuvaan specially focuses its activities to inculcate social sensitivity, brotherhood, love for the country and a humane perspective without discrimination. Yuvaan aims to build up the nation by building up character.
