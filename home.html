OUR STORY PROJECTS ABOUT US

Our mission is to provide educational support, career & employment guidance, vocational skills training, lodging & meals free of cost to socially and economically underprivileged & orphaned students.

Our focus of activities is to inculcate self-respect, confidence, social sensitivity, brotherhood & love for the country. Every youth should have an equal opportunity for higher education.

Our goal is to build up the nation by strengthening character & nurture humane perspective without any discrimination. Every youth should have employment regardless of their social-economic status.
